# Summitline Outfitters - In-Store Pickup FAQ

**Document ID:** kb-3
**Category:** Fulfillment

Online orders can be picked up at any Summitline retail location within
**7 days** of the ready-for-pickup email. After 7 days the order is
automatically returned to the fulfillment center and refunded.

## What to bring

- Government-issued photo ID matching the name on the order.
- The order number from your confirmation email (QR code also works).

## Designated pickup windows

| Location        | Pickup hours              |
| --------------- | ------------------------- |
| Boulder, CO     | Daily 10:00 - 20:00       |
| Bend, OR        | Daily 09:00 - 19:00       |
| Asheville, NC   | Mon-Sat 10:00 - 19:00     |
| Burlington, VT  | Tue-Sun 10:00 - 18:00     |

## Third-party pickup

You may authorize a third party to pick up your order by replying to the
ready-for-pickup email with their full name. They must present their own
photo ID plus a printed or forwarded copy of the authorization email.
