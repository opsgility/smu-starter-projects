# Summitline Outfitters - Lifetime Warranty on Tents

**Document ID:** kb-2
**Category:** Warranty

All Summitline-branded tents carry a **lifetime warranty** against
manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship. The warranty covers
the original purchaser and is non-transferable.

## What is covered

- Seam failure, pole fractures from defective materials, and zipper failure
  due to manufacturing defect.
- Waterproof membrane delamination on our Alpine 4S, Ridgeline, and Basecamp
  tent lines.

## What is not covered

- Damage from normal wear, UV degradation after extended exposure, or
  improper setup.
- Damage from misuse (bear punctures, vehicle contact, stove damage).
- Cosmetic blemishes that do not affect structural integrity.

## How to file a warranty claim

Email `warranty@summitlineoutfitters.com` with (a) your original order
number, (b) photos of the defect, and (c) a short description of the
failure. The warranty team responds within 3 business days.
