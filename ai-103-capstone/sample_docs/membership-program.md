# Summitline Outfitters — Members Program

Summitline Members pay **$35 once** (lifetime) for permanent access to our member benefits. There is no annual fee and no auto-renewal.

## Benefits

- **10% back** in Summitline Dividends on every purchase. Dividends post annually in March and can be redeemed in-store or online.
- **Free shipping** on every order, no minimum.
- **Free in-store services** — boot fitting, pack fitting, beacon check, and 30-minute trip planning consultations.
- **Member-only early access** to new-product drops 48 hours before public release.
- **Gear swap** — trade in gently-used Summitline-brand gear for store credit based on condition and age.
- **2-year extended warranty** available on any product over $200 (see Warranty Policy).

## Not included

- Membership does NOT grant access to Summitline guided trips — those are booked separately through summitline.example/guides.
- Membership discount does not stack with sale pricing. You still earn Dividends on sale purchases.

## Gifting

You can gift a Summitline membership at checkout for $35. The recipient receives an email with a redemption code that never expires.

## Cancellation

Because membership is a one-time lifetime purchase, there is nothing to cancel. Members may request a refund within 30 days of sign-up if they have not yet earned any Dividends.
