# Summitline Alpenglow 24L Daypack

The Alpenglow 24L is Summitline's flagship day-hike and travel pack. At 24 liters it carries everything you need for a full day on the trail — water, layers, lunch, first aid — without the bulk of a full backpacking pack.

- **Price**: $129
- **Weight**: 820 g (1 lb 13 oz)
- **Capacity**: 24 L
- **Main fabric**: Recycled 210D ripstop nylon with PFC-free DWR
- **Frame**: Removable HDPE sheet + aluminum stay
- **Hydration**: Internal sleeve fits 3L reservoirs (sold separately)
- **Colorways**: Granite, Alpine Moss, Canyon Orange
- **Best for**: Day hikes, casual climbing approaches, urban commuting, carry-on travel

## Features

- Dual water-bottle pockets fit 1L Nalgenes.
- Front stretch stash pocket for a shell or a wet layer.
- Floating top lid with a zippered valuables pocket.
- Ice-axe loop + daisy chain for a helmet or trekking poles.
- Padded laptop sleeve fits a 15-inch MacBook Pro.
