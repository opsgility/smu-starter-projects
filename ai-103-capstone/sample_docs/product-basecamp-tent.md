# Summitline Basecamp 3 Tent

The Basecamp 3 is our four-season, three-person expedition tent built for guided Summitline trips in the Tetons, the Cascades, and the Alaska Range.

- **Price**: $649
- **Packaged weight**: 3.6 kg (7 lb 15 oz)
- **Floor area**: 3.9 m^2 / 42 sq ft
- **Poles**: DAC Featherlite NSL aluminum, 4-pole dome
- **Fly**: 30D ripstop nylon, silicone/PU 2000 mm
- **Floor**: 70D nylon, silicone/PU 5000 mm
- **Colorways**: Summit Orange (hi-vis), Slate

## When to pick it

- Winter and shoulder-season mountaineering where you need snow-shedding walls and a bombproof vestibule.
- Guided group trips — three adults fit comfortably with room for boots and one pack in the vestibule.
- Any trip where weight matters less than durability and weather protection.

## Not the right pick when

- You are weight-counting grams for an ultralight traverse (see the Summitline Ridge 2 UL instead — 1.3 kg).
- You are car-camping casually in fair weather (the Summitline Meadow 4 family tent is friendlier and cheaper).

## Accessories

- Basecamp 3 footprint: $59. Recommended on glacial moraine sites.
- Basecamp 3 gear loft: $34. Keeps headlamps and snacks off the floor.
