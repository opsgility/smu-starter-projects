# Summitline Outfitters — Return Policy

Summitline Outfitters accepts returns on unused, undamaged gear within **60 days** of the original purchase date. Items must include original tags and packaging.

## Eligibility

- Unused items in original condition may be returned for a full refund to the original payment method.
- Lightly used items may be exchanged for store credit at our discretion within 30 days.
- Final-sale items (marked on the product page) are not eligible for return.
- Custom-fitted boots and harnesses are non-returnable once fitted, but are covered by our 12-month fit guarantee — we will re-fit or exchange them free of charge.

## How to start a return

1. Visit summitline.example/returns and enter your order number (format `SUM-XXXXXX`).
2. Print the prepaid return label.
3. Drop the package at any FedEx location.

Refunds post to the original payment method within 7 business days of warehouse receipt.

## Exceptions

Hazardous goods (fuel canisters, bear spray, avalanche airbag cartridges) cannot be shipped back and must be returned in-store. Your local Summitline store will exchange or refund on the spot.
