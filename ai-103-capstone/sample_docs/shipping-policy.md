# Summitline Outfitters — Shipping

## Domestic (US)

- **Standard** ground (3–5 business days): free on orders $75+, otherwise $7.95.
- **Expedited** 2-day: $14.95.
- **Overnight**: $29.95 (order by 1 pm MT).

Orders typically ship the same business day. You'll receive a tracking email when your order leaves our Denver fulfillment center.

## International

We ship to Canada, the UK, Germany, France, Japan, and Australia. International orders are quoted at checkout. Duties and taxes are the customer's responsibility.

## Order status

Every Summitline order is assigned an identifier of the form `SUM-######`. To check status:

- Visit summitline.example/orders/SUM-###### — live status, carrier, and estimated arrival.
- Email support@summitline.example with the order ID.
- Ask the in-store kiosk or chat concierge — it can look up any order by ID.

## Carriers

Most orders ship via FedEx Ground. Overnight orders ship via FedEx Priority Overnight. Orders to PO boxes ship via USPS Priority.

## Lost or damaged packages

File a claim within 14 days of the expected delivery date. We will reship at no cost once FedEx confirms the loss.
