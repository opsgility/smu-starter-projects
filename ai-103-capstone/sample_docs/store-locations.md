# Summitline Outfitters — Store Locations & Services

## Flagship stores

- **Boulder, CO** — 2480 Pearl Street. Boot-fit lab, climbing harness fit, repair intake. Open 9 am–8 pm daily.
- **Seattle, WA** — 1919 2nd Avenue. Ski tuning in winter; rental fleet for Mt. Rainier. Open 9 am–8 pm daily.
- **Burlington, VT** — 180 Church Street. Paddle rentals in summer; Nordic ski rentals in winter. Open 10 am–7 pm daily.
- **Salt Lake City, UT** — 602 East 500 South. Avalanche education classroom; beacon / shovel / probe demos every Saturday. Open 9 am–8 pm daily.

## In-store services

Every flagship store offers the following at no charge for Summitline members:

- **Pack fitting** — we measure torso length and hip circumference and swap in the right harness size.
- **Boot fitting** — heat-moldable liners, footbed fitting, and up to two free aftercare visits per pair.
- **Beacon check** — we test your avalanche transceiver's send and receive frequencies before every season.
- **Trip planning** — 30-minute one-on-one with a Summitline guide for members planning a trip to a Summitline-serviced range.

## Kiosk voice concierge

Every flagship store has a Summitline AI voice kiosk near the entrance. Customers can ask it about product availability, store hours, class schedules, and their own order status (just speak the order ID, e.g. "SUM-884210"). Kiosks are powered by the same Summitline platform that answers the website chat and runs the support phone line.

## Non-flagship locations

Summitline also operates 22 smaller seasonal stores near major trailheads and ski resorts. Those stores carry a subset of the catalog and do not offer boot-fitting or repair intake. See summitline.example/stores for the full list.
