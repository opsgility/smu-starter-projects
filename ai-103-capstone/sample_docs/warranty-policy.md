# Summitline Outfitters — Warranty & Repair

Every piece of Summitline-branded gear carries a **limited lifetime warranty** against defects in materials and workmanship. Third-party brands carry the manufacturer's warranty — we'll help you file the claim.

## What's covered

- Seam failures, zipper failures, and fabric delamination on Summitline apparel and packs.
- Buckle, webbing, and hardware failures on harnesses (within the product's retirement window).
- Factory defects on Summitline tents (poles, clips, flies) for the life of the original owner.

## What's not covered

- Normal wear and tear, abrasion from rock / rope friction, or UV damage from prolonged sun exposure.
- Damage from improper cleaning or unauthorized repairs.
- Cosmetic wear on boots after 90 days of use.

## Extended warranty

Summitline members can purchase a **2-year extended warranty** on any product over $200 at checkout or within 30 days of purchase. Extended warranty covers accidental damage in addition to manufacturing defects.

## Repairs

Our Boulder repair center handles zipper swaps, buckle replacements, and seam re-stitching at cost. Request a repair RMA at summitline.example/repair and ship the item in. Typical turnaround is 2–3 weeks in peak season.
