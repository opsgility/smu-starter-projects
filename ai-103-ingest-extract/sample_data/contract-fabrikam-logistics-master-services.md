# Master Services Agreement - Fabrikam Logistics, Inc.

**Vendor:** Fabrikam Logistics, Inc.
**Counterparty:** Summitline Outfitters, Inc.
**Document type:** Contract
**Contract number:** MSA-FBR-2026-003
**Effective date:** 2026-01-15
**Initial term:** 24 months
**Annual contract value:** 186,000.00 USD

## Parties

This Master Services Agreement (the Agreement) is entered into between
Fabrikam Logistics, Inc. (Provider) and Summitline Outfitters, Inc.
(Customer) as of the Effective Date noted above.

## Scope of services

Provider will perform inbound and outbound third-party logistics (3PL)
services for Customer Pacific Northwest distribution footprint, including:

- Cross-dock operations at the Auburn, WA and Hillsboro, OR hubs
- Parcel and LTL freight brokerage
- Returns processing for the Summitline e-commerce channel
- Inventory cycle counts on a 90-day cadence

## Service levels

- Same-day ship cutoff: 3:00 PM local
- On-time delivery (ground): at least 97.0 percent
- Inventory accuracy: at least 99.5 percent
- Returns processing turnaround: at most 3 business days

## Pricing

Base fee: 15,500.00 USD per month. Activity-based charges (per-parcel,
per-pallet, per-return) are billed monthly in arrears at the rate card in
Exhibit A. Total anticipated annual spend at forecasted volumes: 186,000.00 USD.

## Payment terms

Net 30 from invoice date. Late payments accrue interest at 1.0 percent per
month, not to exceed the maximum rate permitted by Washington law.

## Term and termination

Initial term of 24 months beginning 2026-01-15, auto-renewing for successive
12-month terms unless either party gives 90 days written notice. Either party
may terminate for uncured material breach on 30 days written notice.

## Summary

Two-year 3PL master services agreement with Fabrikam Logistics for cross-dock,
freight brokerage, and returns handling across the Auburn and Hillsboro hubs,
at a base fee of 15,500.00 USD per month and an estimated total contract value
of **186,000.00 USD**.
