# Supply Agreement - Northwind Gear Works, LLC

**Vendor:** Northwind Gear Works, LLC
**Counterparty:** Summitline Outfitters, Inc.
**Document type:** Contract
**Contract number:** SA-NWG-2026-011
**Effective date:** 2026-02-01
**Initial term:** 12 months
**Committed spend:** 420,000.00 USD

## Parties

This Supply Agreement is entered into between Northwind Gear Works, LLC
(Supplier) and Summitline Outfitters, Inc. (Buyer) on the Effective Date above.

## Products covered

Supplier will manufacture and deliver the following Summitline private-label
products at the quantities and pricing set forth in Exhibit A:

- SL-BAG-GLC15 Glacier 15F down sleeping bags (8,000 units)
- SL-BAG-GLC30 Glacier 30F down sleeping bags (6,000 units)
- SL-BAG-LINER silk bag liners (12,000 units)

## Pricing and volume

Base unit prices are locked for the 12-month term, subject to a single cost
adjustment if the 850-fill-power down index moves more than plus or minus 8
percent, measured against the IDFL March 2026 settlement.

Committed spend across all SKUs: **420,000.00 USD** over 12 months.

## Quality and inspection

Supplier warrants that each lot will conform to the Summitline Quality Manual
rev 4.2, including EN/ISO 23537 temperature rating validation on a 1-in-500
sample basis. Buyer may inspect at Supplier facility with 10 business days
notice.

## Payment terms

Net 45 from receipt of conforming goods. Early-payment discount of 1.5 percent
available for payment within 10 days.

## Term and termination

Initial term ends 2027-01-31. Either party may terminate for cause on 30 days
written notice; for convenience on 120 days written notice.

## Summary

12-month private-label supply agreement with Northwind Gear Works for 14,000
Glacier down sleeping bags and 12,000 bag liners at a committed spend of
**420,000.00 USD**, Net 45 with a 1.5 percent early-payment discount.
