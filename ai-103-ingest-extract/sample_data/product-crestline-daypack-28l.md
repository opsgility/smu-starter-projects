# Crestline 28L Daypack - Product Data Sheet

**Vendor:** Ironseam Packs LLC
**Product SKU:** SL-PACK-CR28-2026
**Document type:** Product data sheet
**Revision date:** 2026-02-02

## Summary

The Crestline 28L is Summitline's do-everything technical daypack. Built
from recycled 210D high-tenacity nylon with a frame sheet and 6 mm
HDPE stays, it carries up to 11 kg comfortably on long day hikes, sport
climbs at the crag, or a full day of resort skiing.

## Warranty

Lifetime warranty against defects in materials and workmanship for the
original owner. Damage from ice-axe self-arrest, ski-edge cuts, or
airline handling is not covered but can be repaired for a flat fee of
$45 under the Summitline Rebuild Program.
