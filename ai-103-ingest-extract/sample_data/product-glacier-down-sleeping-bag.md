# Glacier 15F Down Sleeping Bag - Product Data Sheet

**Document type:** Product data sheet
**Revision date:** 2025-11-30

## Summary

The Glacier 15F is Summitline's three-season down mummy bag. It is filled
with 850-fill-power ethically sourced goose down treated with a durable
water-repellent (DWR) finish for wet-weather resilience, and is rated to
an EN/ISO 23537 comfort limit of -9 C (15 F).

Limited lifetime warranty on seam construction, zipper hardware, and
baffle integrity for the original owner. Down migration due to improper
storage (e.g. long-term compression) is not covered.
