## Summary

The Ridgeline Hardshell is a three-layer waterproof-breathable mountain
shell tuned for alpine climbing and ski mountaineering in the Cascades.
It combines a 3L recycled nylon face with a 20,000 mm / 20,000 g/m^2/24h
membrane and fully-taped seams, delivering storm protection at a trim
395 g (men's medium).
