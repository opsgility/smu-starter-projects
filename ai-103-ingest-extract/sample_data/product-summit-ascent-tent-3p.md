## Summary

The Summit Ascent 3P is Summitline Outfitters' flagship three-person,
four-season mountaineering tent. It pairs a 20D ripstop silicone-PU fly
with a fully-taped bathtub floor to deliver a 2,500 mm hydrostatic head
while weighing just 2.14 kg trail weight.
