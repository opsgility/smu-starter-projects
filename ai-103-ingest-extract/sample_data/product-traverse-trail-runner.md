# Traverse Trail Runner - Product Data Sheet

**Vendor:** Switchback Footwear Co.
**Product SKU:** SL-SHOE-TRV-2026
**Document type:** Product data sheet
**Revision date:** 2026-03-05

## Summary

The Traverse Trail Runner is Summitline's do-everything trail shoe, built
for long days on mixed Pacific Northwest terrain. A 28 mm supercritical-foam
midsole and Vibram Megagrip outsole give it both the cushion for ultra
efforts and the bite for wet granite slab.

## Warranty

12-month manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and
workmanship. Outsole wear below 2 mm, midsole compaction from normal use,
and crash damage are not covered.
