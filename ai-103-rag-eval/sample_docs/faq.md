# Summitline Outfitters — Frequently Asked Questions

## Account & orders

**Do I need an account to buy?** No, guest checkout is available, but creating
an account is free and lets you track orders, save addresses, and start
returns online.

**Can I change or cancel an order after it is placed?** Yes, within **60
minutes** of placing it — open the order in Order History and click
**Cancel**. After 60 minutes the order is released to the warehouse and
cannot be cancelled.

**Do you price-match?** Summitline price-matches authorized retailers
(including REI, Backcountry, and the manufacturer's own site) within 7 days
of your purchase. Submit a Concierge ticket with a link to the lower price
and we will refund the difference to your original payment method.

## Payments

Summitline accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, Apple Pay,
Google Pay, PayPal, and Summitline Gift Cards. Purchase orders are available
for corporate accounts — contact the Concierge team to set one up.

## Memberships

The **Summitline Trail Pass** is a free loyalty program. Members earn 5%
back on every purchase as store credit, get early access to sale events, and
pay no shipping on orders over $50. Sign up from your account dashboard.

## Product questions

**Are your down products ethically sourced?** Yes. All Summitline down
products use **Responsible Down Standard (RDS) certified** down. Certification
details are printed on each product tag and listed on the product page.

**Do you offer gear rentals?** Yes — Summitline rents tents, sleeping bags,
bear canisters, and avalanche safety kits from our Salt Lake City and Denver
flagship stores. Reservations can be made up to 90 days in advance.

**Is live chat available?** Yes, Summitline Concierge live chat is available
7 days a week, 6 a.m. to 10 p.m. Mountain Time.
