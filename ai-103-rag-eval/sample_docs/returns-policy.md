# Summitline Outfitters Return Policy

Summitline Outfitters accepts returns on most gear within **30 days** of the
delivery date. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and include the
original receipt or order number. Returns initiated after 30 days are handled
case-by-case by the Summitline Concierge team and are not guaranteed.

## How to start a return

1. Log in to your Summitline account and open the order in **Order History**.
2. Click **Return or exchange**, pick the items and a reason, and submit.
3. Print the prepaid UPS return label that is emailed to you.
4. Drop the package at any UPS location within 14 days of creating the label.

## Refund timing

Refunds process within **7 business days** after the warehouse scans your
return. The refund goes back to the original payment method. Gift-card orders
are refunded as store credit.

## Non-returnable items

The following categories are final sale and cannot be returned:

- Avalanche beacons and safety electronics that have been activated.
- Climbing hardware (carabiners, cams, nuts) once the tamper seal is broken.
- Food, fuel canisters, and perishables.
- Custom-embroidered or monogrammed apparel.

## Damaged or defective gear

If your order arrives damaged, contact Summitline Support within **48 hours**
of delivery. Photograph the damage and include the photos in your ticket — we
will ship a replacement from the nearest fulfillment center and include a
prepaid label for the damaged item.
