# Summitline Outfitters Shipping Policy

Summitline ships to all 50 U.S. states, Canada, and most of the EU from three
fulfillment centers in Salt Lake City, Charlotte, and Portland.

## Standard shipping

- **Orders under $75**: flat $6.95 shipping, 3-5 business day delivery.
- **Orders $75 and above**: free standard shipping, 3-5 business day delivery.
- Most orders leave the warehouse within 1 business day of being placed.

## Expedited options

- **2-Day Air**: $14.95 (free on orders over $200).
- **Next-Day Air**: $29.95, cutoff 2 p.m. Mountain Time.

## International shipping

International orders ship via DHL with duties and taxes calculated at
checkout. Transit time is typically 5-10 business days. Hazardous materials
(fuel canisters, bear spray, avalanche airbag cartridges) cannot ship
internationally.

## Backorders

When an item is on backorder, Summitline ships the rest of the order
immediately and sends the backordered item separately at no additional charge
when stock arrives. You receive a tracking number for each shipment.

## Tracking

Tracking numbers are emailed the moment a package leaves the warehouse and
are also visible under **Order History** in your Summitline account.
