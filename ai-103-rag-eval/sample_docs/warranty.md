# Summitline Outfitters Gear Warranty

Every piece of Summitline-branded gear is covered by the **Summitline Lifetime
Trail Warranty**. If a Summitline product fails due to a manufacturing defect
or a workmanship flaw at any point in its usable life, Summitline will repair
it, replace it, or refund the original purchase price at its discretion.

## What the warranty covers

- Stitching failures, zipper failures, and fabric delamination on Summitline
  apparel, packs, and shelters.
- Broken buckles, straps, and load-bearing hardware on Summitline packs and
  harnesses.
- Defective electronics on Summitline-branded headlamps, satellite messengers,
  and GPS watches within 2 years of purchase.

## What the warranty does NOT cover

- Normal wear and tear (abraded fabric, UV-faded color, worn-out footwear
  soles).
- Damage from improper care (machine-drying technical fabrics, storing a wet
  tent, etc.).
- Loss, theft, or third-party modifications.
- Products from partner brands sold by Summitline — those are covered by the
  original manufacturer's warranty.

## Filing a warranty claim

1. Open a Summitline Concierge ticket with the order number, a description
   of the failure, and photos showing the defect.
2. Summitline replies within 2 business days with a prepaid shipping label.
3. Ship the item to the Salt Lake City repair center.
4. Repair turnaround is typically 14-21 business days; replacements ship
   within 5 business days of inspection.

For partner-brand products, Summitline Support will forward your claim to the
manufacturer and track it on your behalf.
