# Capstone sample assets

For a realistic demo, drop into this folder:
- `sample.jpg` — a retail product photo (< 200 KB) for /vision.
- `sample.wav` — a short spoken question (< 5 s) for /transcribe.
- `sample_invoice.md` or `sample_invoice.pdf` — a short invoice for /extract.

The capstone instructions show you how to generate each from earlier exercises.
