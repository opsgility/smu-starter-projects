# Content Understanding sample data

During the lab you'll download sample assets to this folder:

- `invoice.pdf` — a simple supplier invoice (the lab links to one you can download)
- `receipt.jpg` — a retail receipt image
- `support_call.wav` — a short support-call recording
- `training.mp4` — a short training video (under 2 minutes keeps the lab inside the time budget)

Each corresponds to one of the four `extract_*.py` scripts.
