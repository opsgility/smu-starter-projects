# Responsible AI Rubric

Score each scenario against Microsoft's six Responsible AI principles.

| Principle | Question to ask |
|---|---|
| Fairness | Does the system produce consistent results across demographic groups? |
| Reliability & Safety | Does the system operate reliably under edge cases and stress? |
| Privacy & Security | Is personal data minimized, protected, and auditable? |
| Inclusiveness | Does the system work for people with different abilities / backgrounds? |
| Transparency | Can users understand how and why decisions are made? |
| Accountability | Is there a named owner and escalation path for issues? |

## Content Safety severity levels

- 0: Safe
- 2: Low
- 4: Medium
- 6: High

The exercise treats severity >= 2 as a block.
