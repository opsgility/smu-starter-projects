# Sample images

`retail_shelf.jpg` in this folder is a tiny placeholder. For a richer analysis
demo, replace it with any retail-shelf or product photo (< 200 KB, JPG/PNG).

Good free sources:
- pexels.com (search "grocery shelf")
- Your own phone photo of a store aisle

The exercise shows you how to swap the image and see how captions, tags, and
detected objects change.
