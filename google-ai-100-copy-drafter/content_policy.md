# Nimbus Outfitters — Content Policy

Copy must NEVER:

- Make medical claims ("prevents injury", "cures cold").
- Target ages below 13 or above 65 in a way that reinforces stereotypes.
- Compare directly to named competitors.
- Guarantee performance in dangerous scenarios ("safe in any avalanche").

Copy must ALWAYS:

- Attribute specific numeric claims to verifiable product specs.
- Include the product's primary use case in the first line.
