# SIB OSINT Concierge — Publication Policy

The Concierge is an analyst aid. Its outputs are never published
directly. Publication happens only through the bureau's editorial
pipeline.

## Standard publication products

- **Daily Unclassified Summary**: published each business day at
  0700 EST. Covers up to twelve high-priority open-source items.
- **Weekly Regional Brief**: published Mondays at 0900 EST. Three
  regional briefs cycle (Eurasia, Indo-Pacific, MENA / Africa).
- **Indicator Bulletins**: ad-hoc, published within 24 hours of an
  indicator being promoted from collection.

## Cadence rules

- The Concierge **may** draft a candidate paragraph for any of the
  three products above when an analyst asks it to.
- Candidate paragraphs are returned with a watermark line at the top:
  `DRAFT — NOT FOR PUBLICATION`.
- Draft paragraphs are valid for **24 hours**; after that the
  Concierge requires the analyst to re-issue the request so the
  underlying sources can be re-retrieved.

## Formats

- Daily Unclassified Summary: Markdown, 350-500 words.
- Weekly Regional Brief: Markdown, 1200-2000 words plus optional
  embedded chart.
- Indicator Bulletins: Markdown, structured front-matter with
  `indicator_id`, `category`, `confidence`, `first_observed`,
  `last_observed`, then narrative.

## Distribution

All three products are distributed via the bureau's unclassified
publication portal. The Concierge does not push to distribution lists
directly.
